Tissues

National Paper Industries Co. (NAPICO) is the oldest and biggest tissue paper converting factory in the State of Qatar. Established in 1991, as a branch of Qatar Industrial Manufacturing Company, started its operations with the converting of Facial Tissues.

Gradually over the years, Napico have increased its production capabilities and added other related products such as Toilet Rolls, Kitchen Towels, Maxi Rolls, C Fold and Wet Wipes.
